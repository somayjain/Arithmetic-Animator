 USER-GUIDE
As soon as you open index.html you are asked to select between the illustration and exercise.
Then you choose a animation which can be changed later.
Then you are prompted to selected among the multiplications in the list.
In illustration by clicking “Next” button the next step is shown with a voice in the background.
The “Undo” button takes you to the previous step.
If exercise was chosen earlier, you are redirected to the exercise page. 
You have to do some of the things manually in the exercise part. Such as selecteing which row or column is to be transposed etc. 
An “Undo”  option is provided at the bottom.
An “Hint” option is available at any stage in exercises.
Finally answer needs to be entered.
